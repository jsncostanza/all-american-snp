how it started

A neighborhood shop.

All American Sandwiches N Pizza started with a simple idea: serve the kind of food we'd want our own family to eat. Pizza with dough we make ourselves. Sandwiches stacked the way you'd stack them at home, generous, fresh, and never skimped on.

Years later, that's still what we do. Same recipes, same care, same friendly faces behind the counter.

what we believe

Honest food.
Real ingredients.

Good food doesn't need to be complicated, it needs to be honest. We mix our dough by hand. We slice our meats and cheeses fresh. We use real ingredients because there's no shortcut for that.

And we treat everyone who walks in like family, whether you're a regular who orders the same Italian sub every Friday or it's your very first time stopping by.

what we stand for

Three Promises

Fresh, Every Day

Dough made in-house each morning. Produce delivered fresh. If it's on your plate, it was made today.

Generous Portions

You're going to leave full. We've never been the kind of place that nickel-and-dimes a slice or a sandwich.

Real Hospitality

We know our regulars by name and we treat newcomers like ones-in-waiting. It's how a neighborhood shop should feel.
